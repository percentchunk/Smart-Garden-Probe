## Cost Notes

The prices above use the current listed purchase price of each linked product or package. Some products are sold in packs even though only a few pieces are required for one Smart Garden Probe.

The project itself only uses three switches, three keycaps, four screws, and four heat-set inserts, so the per-device material cost is lower than the full checkout total. Shipping and taxes are not included because they depend on the supplier and delivery location.

Parts such as hookup wire, solder, and filament are listed according to the approximate amount consumed by one build rather than the cost of purchasing an entire spool or roll.
